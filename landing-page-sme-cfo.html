OakNorth reported £223 million pre-tax profit in 2025, an efficiency ratio of 26%, and 0.045% cumulative principal losses over its 10-year operating history. Those numbers are better than most Big Four branch-level credit books would prefer to advertise.

Switching banks disrupts our direct debits and supplier payment rails.

You do not need to switch your primary current account to use OakNorth for business savings. OakNorth savings accounts are standalone — you keep your Barclays or HSBC current account for daily operations. Your reserves earn 2.35% to 3.80% AER at OakNorth depending on the product. There is no operational disruption to your payment infrastructure.

Our auditors will ask why we moved cash to a smaller bank.

OakNorth is a fully regulated UK bank — not an e-money institution or fintech app. The audit trail is clean: PRA authorisation, FCA regulation, FSCS protection, published audited accounts. If your auditor asks, the answer is: higher yield, equivalent FSCS protection, equivalent regulatory status. A 1.75 percentage point rate advantage over Barclays is a commercially sound treasury decision, not an anomaly to explain.

I need a relationship manager I can call, not just an app.

OakNorth assigns dedicated relationship managers to Business Banking clients. The lending model is explicitly relationship-based — which is why 40%+ of OakNorth borrowers return for subsequent facilities. This is not a call centre model. You speak to someone who knows your company and your sector.

The minimum is £100,000. We don't have that sitting idle.

The £100,000 minimum applies to the Business Notice Account. If your current operating reserves are below that threshold, Fixed Term Deposits and notice accounts have different minimums.
