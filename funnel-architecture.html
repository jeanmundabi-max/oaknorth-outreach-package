Revenue Model — Why Fixing the Funnel Compounds

Every deposit account opened directly is an account OakNorth owns. No broker margin. No comparison site dependency.

£10,600

Annual yield gap per SME on £500k

OakNorth Notice 125-day at 3.62% AER versus Barclays Business at ~1.5% AER. This is the arithmetic argument the CFO cannot dispute once they see it. On a database of 25 target companies, the aggregate opportunity is £265,000 per year in incremental client yield — that is the switching incentive. (Rates verified oaknorth.co.uk/business-banking/savings/ May 2026.)

80%+

Lending from broker referrals

Every broker referral carries a margin cost. Every direct relationship opened through outbound is acquired at lower cost and with deeper OakNorth ownership. A 20-point reduction in broker dependence (80% to 60%) changes the lending unit economics permanently — without reducing total volume.

£35k to £50k

AP/AR manager salary eliminated per Monite signup

23+ UK companies in OakNorth's target sectors are actively hiring Accounts Payable or Accounts Receivable managers right now. Each posting signals a Finance Director about to spend £35,000 to £50,000 on headcount to solve a problem OakNorth's Monite product eliminates. OakNorth acquires the customer and saves the CFO a hire.

0%

CAC on existing borrower cross-sell

OakNorth has a 40%+ repeat borrower rate and thousands of existing lending relationships that have never been offered Business Earn Vault savings. A single email campaign to existing borrowers with operating cash at incumbent banks has near-zero acquisition cost and reaches the warmest possible audience — people who already trust OakNorth to hold their debt.

3 Growth Feedback Loops

How direct acquisition compounds. Each loop reinforces the next — the channel gets cheaper and stronger with every account opened.

Loop 1

Direct Deposit Acquisition Loop

1

MDB identifies CFOs with operating cash above £100k using Companies House signals and FSCS switching probability score

2

Outbound email sequence leads with £10,600 yield gap argument (3.62% Notice vs ~1.5% Barclays on £500k) — arithmetic, not marketing language

3

CFO opens Business Earn Vault. OakNorth earns the deposit relationship directly — no broker, no comparison site

4

Named CFO becomes case study. Case study becomes social proof on the acquisition landing page

5

Landing page converts the next cohort of CFOs at higher rates because proof now exists. CAC decreases per account.

Loop 2

Broker Relationship Compound Loop

1

MDB builds NACFB broker engagement programme — Monite as new recommendation capability for their clients

2

Broker adds OakNorth to active panel. Considers OakNorth on every qualifying deal (average 3 to 5 per year)

3

OakNorth wins broker-referred deal. Borrower also presented with Business Earn Vault cross-sell

4

Borrower opens deposit account. OakNorth earns the full relationship (lending + savings) from one broker referral

5

OakNorth's case study from that relationship gives the next broker a concrete reason to recommend. Loop compounds.

Loop 3

Authority Engine Trust Loop

1

LinkedIn content flywheel publishes CFO-relevant insight: FSCS calculator, Allica vs OakNorth comparison, Monite AP/AR automation primer

2

CFOs engage with content. Those not yet ready to switch enter the 20-week value-only nurture sequence

3

Lead scoring tracks intent signals. When a CFO crosses the hot threshold, OakNorth commercial team receives an alert

4

Commercial team calls a CFO who already understands the FSCS argument, has seen the yield calculator, and has mentally evaluated OakNorth. Conversion rates 3x a cold call.

5

Converted CFO shares OakNorth insight content with their network. Organic reach compounds. Loop starts again.
